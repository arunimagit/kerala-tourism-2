palakkad

State to the rest of the country through the Palakkad Gap with a width of 32 to 40 Kms. Its geographical position, historical background, educational status, tourism hot-spots and above all, the development activities that are carried out, are wide and varied. The district is one of the main granaries Palakkad is one of the fourteen districts of Kerala and has no coastal line. The district opens the of Kerala and its economy is primarily agricultural. The district is also the land of Palmyrahs.
